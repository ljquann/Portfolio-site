About Me

After 13 years of healthcare experience, I have made the move web development and design. I have worked as a Registered Nurse specializing in hospice, emergency, and case management.

While my career in nursing did not provide experience in web development, it did provide daily practice in:

  • Prioritization
  • Observation & Assessment
  • Interprofessional Collaboration & Communication
  • Complex Problem-Solving
  • User Empathy

I was able to master these skills while keeping the individual needs of my patients central. These skills are highly applicable to creating influential web content.

The logic problems and creative aspects of web development and design attracted me to a new career in tech. I have made a curriculum to follow throughout the year of 2021 based on coding bootcamps syllabi. I am motivated to collaborate with small businesses and non-profits locally for coding and design projects and plan to contribute to open-source projects for experience.

I am currently proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am learning React.
